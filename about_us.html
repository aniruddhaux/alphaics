  • About Us

AlphaICs has developed a Next-generation AI Processor, called Real AI Processor (RAPTM ), based on a proprietary architecture. The RAPTM architecture makes use of a specialized Instruction Set Architecture (ISA), designed for accelerating AI workloads. RAPTM is highly scalable and can be scaled for mobile devices to datacenter solutions.

Leadership

  • Pradeep VajramCEO & Exec. Chairman

    Pradeep Vajram CEO & Exec. Chairman

    Pradeep Vajram is a successful entrepreneur and a veteran in the Semiconductor / Embedded industry. He has over 25+ years of experience in having executed, at all levels of responsibilities in design and development of ASIC products.

    Pradeep was the President of Semiconductor and Embedded Systesm Business Unit at Aricent until September 2016. Pradeep founded SmartPlay Technologies in 2008 – the world’s first integrated end-to-end product engineering services company. SmartPlay has been ranked the 2nd largest VLSI services company in India also ranked among the Top 50 Best Companies to Work in the GPTW IT/BT category. SmartPlay was acquired by Aricent in 2015.

    Prior to SmartPlay, he served as the Vice President of Engineering at Qualcomm, heading the India semiconductor division (Bangalore). Under his leadership, Qualcomm Bangalore Design Center developed as a strong center of excellence and delivered multiple 3G/4G products successfully. Pradeep played a key role in helping setup the Bangalore Design Centre (India) for Qualcomm.

    Prior to Qualcomm, Pradeep was the COO & co-founder of Spike Technologies – a leading chip design services company. Spike was acquired by Qualcomm in 2004. Pradeep also worked at LSI Logic in Milpitas, CA.

    Pradeep has a Bachelor’s degree in Electronics Engineering from Karnataka University & a Masters degree in Computer Engineering from Wayne State University, Detroit (USA).

  • Sanjay Palsamudram COO & India MD

    Sanjay Palsamudram COO & India MD

    Sanjay has over 25+ years of experience in the Semiconductor, Wireless, and Product Engineering/R&D Services Industry. Before joining AlphaICs, Sanjay was working as a CEO of Yana Mobility, a leading embedded systems, and electronics company that develops and manufactures automotive products for Indian and European auto OEMs.

    As CEO, he leads the business strategy, customer acquisition, product definition, and engineering execution of diagnostics and telematics products for connected car solutions. Prior to Yana, he was the co-founder and COO of Smartplay Technologies, a 1200+ employees-product engineering services company, that was acquired by Aricent. He led business P&L, operations, and eng. execution of Semiconductor BU – both digital and analog. He spearheaded EDA methodology, SoC turnkey development, and IP solutions business at SmartPlay, and was responsible for foundry interface, EDA contracts, and engineering execution; delivering multiple customer products from RTL to GDS2.

    Prior to SmartPlay, he spent 15 years in the US with extensive experience in setting up and managing world-wide operations and product development teams for Texas Instruments and Sun Microsystems. At TI, he was the General Manager of wireless division, responsible for global R&D and delivery of several modems and application processors that powered the top 5 cell phone OEMs. At Sun Microsystems, he held many engineering leadership positions spanning from hand-on lead custom circuit designer to Sr. design manager in delivering four generations of UltraSPARC Microprocessors. He pioneered a pure-play foundry model for silicon sourcing with Taiwan foundries. He was instrumental in bootstrapping and setting up Sun’s “India Engineering Center” working with the Indian Central and State Governments.

    He is also an Angel Investor, investing in deep-technology companies in B2B industry sectors. He serves on the board of several of these companies taking an active part in strategy and high-level operations. He graduated from Arizona State University with a Masters in Electrical Engineering and has an undergraduate degree in Electronics from Bangalore University, India.

  • Prashant Trivedi VP Business Development & Co-founder

    Prashant Trivedi VP Business Development & Co-founder

    Prashant has 16 years of experience in design, development and management of projects in Semiconductors, Technology and Internet – serving several roles in different industry verticals in India and the US.

    After initiating his career as a Semiconductor Design Engineer with a Fab-Less Telecom Company called Transwitch in India, he went on to Cypress Semiconductor, Tejas Networks and Sarnoff India in their Marketing and Product Management divisions. With a strong technical knowledge, problem-solving ability, communication, leadership and project management skills, Prashant excels in improving existing projects and identifying new opportunities.

    In his Sales role in Sarnoff India, he successfully developed sales channels to build closer ties with the Indian Public Sector. His competence in identifying new opportunities and processes to drive quality, efficiency and cost effectiveness soon resulted in his movement to Sarnoff Corporation US with a Business Operations role to acquire new markets.

    In 2008 Prashant co-founded TalkTacit.com, an internet company in the employment media space. His responsibility in TalkTacit.com was to lead design and strategy. The company was later renamed Dhung.com.

    When he joined DuPont in 2010, he was responsible for business strategy development for the company’s foray into Energy Storage where he liaised with automotive companies across the globe. He worked closely with the technology team to initiate material development programs and manage customer relationship. He also worked with DuPont Venture team to form developmental relationships with start-ups in the Energy Storage space. Prashant then co-founded AlphaICS in 2016 with a vision of developing next generation AI compute.

    Prashant has a bachelor’s Degree from IIT Kanpur and he is an alumnus of the ISB Hyderabad.

  • Vinod DhamCo-Founder

    Vinod Dham Co-Founder

    40+ years of experience in developing semiconductor products and technologies, last ten in making investments. Acclaimed worldwide as the “Father of Pentium” for leading one of industry’s most successful brands from inception to its worldwide success.

    Vinod (Vin) is a Founding Managing Director of IndoUS Venture Partners (IUVP), an early stage venture capital fund focused on investing in India. Prior to founding IUVP, Vin received his initial exposure to India by co-founding a cross border incubator, NewPath Ventures LLC.

    Before that, Vin was Chief Executive Officer of Silicon Spice, a chip design start-up he sold to Broadcom for $1.2 billion. Earlier, Vin was Chief Operating Officer at NexGen, a microprocessor design start-up, which was acquired by AMD for $800m.

    Prior to his start-up stints, Vin was Intel’s Vice President of Pentium Processor Division and earlier General Manager of i486 Workgroup Computing Division. During his 16 years at Intel, he successfully managed multi-billion dollar businesses – Intel 386 compaction, i486, and the Pentium Processor families. Earlier he worked on Intel’s Non-Volatile memory products and was a co-inventor of Intel’s Flash technology.

    Vin has received numerous recognitions over the years. Recently Vin has been highlighted amongst the Indian-Americans who have helped shape America, at a first-ever exhibition on South East Asians in the National Museum of Natural History at the storied Smithsonian in Washington DC. In the past he was named as one of the top 25 executives in the computer industry and one of the top 100 most influential Asian Americans.

    Vin has co-authored numerous technical papers and patents. He is a Distinguished Alumnus of both Delhi College of Engineering and University of Cincinnati where he received his Bachelor’s and Master’s Degrees in Electrical Engineering, respectively.
